COGNITO

Project

With Internet of Things (IoT) becoming more common place in everyday devices, the task assigned was to conceptually design and produce an IoT product model. Thus leading to the making of COGNITO: a smart voice and face recognition doorbell. COGNITO was designed with the intention of working with other smart home technology to help increase convenience and security around the home.

Role

3D Modelling, Product and Package Design, Finishing, Wiring

Utilised Platforms

3DSMax, Rhino

The model, 3D printed with PLA filament, was wet sanded down with 360 and 120 grit sandpaper and sprayed with primer filler and spray paint to replicate a smooth end product look. A single led strip and tactile switch was used to mimic the button's intended working effect. Finally, the packaging was etched and laser cut from boxboard, and the mock-up camera lens was laser cut from perspex.
