MINDFULNESS MEDITATION TEDDY

Project

Through the implementation of body scanning mindfulness practices, the mindfulness meditation teddy bear was created with the aim of improving attention span and concentration amongst children.

Team

Jessica Fernandez, Edmond Hua

Role

Development, Programming, Wiring

Utilised Platforms

Arduino IDE, Java

Alongside a calming audio meditation track, mellowing LEDs respectively light up on the teddy bear to instruct the child of the respective area they are to concentrate and focus their attention on.
