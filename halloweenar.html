AR Halloween Card

The focus of this idea was to challenge my Unity and 3D modelling skills by creating an AR Halloween card. This AR Halloween card is viewable via a mobile device, where the card acts as a portal to allow visitors to enter the world presented in the card. The card appears 2D from the front, but actually consists of depth where the visitor can move their device to peer through the portal and view the 3D assets and design from various angles. The 3D assets were modelled in blender, while the scene was constructed via Unity and exported as a WebGL application to allow anyone to access it via a browser via this link here.
