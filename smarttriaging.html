SMART TRIAGING

Project

Smart Triaging is a smartwatch and mobile application design project of designing screen based interfaces for an autonomous technology based concept. The team focused on how autonomous technologies could be used to streamline emergency ambulance services so to meet the ever-growing demand. This resulted in the conception of Smart Triaging: a futuristic triaging smartwatch application that automatically scans the user's body and alerts them of any medical injuries, allowing them to call for an ambulance for that injury straight from their smartwatch.
